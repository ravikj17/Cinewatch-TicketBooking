About Us

Cinewatch is an Online Movie Ticket Booking System which provide the customers facility to book tickets for a movie online and to gather information about the movies and theatre's.

Our objectives

  • To provide the online booking service for movie tickets.
  • To provide information about movies for example- cast ,description ,etc.
  • To provide information about availability of movie tickets at various theaters.
  • To provide special screening of movies on public demand via polling system.
